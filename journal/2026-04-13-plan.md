## Mindset Reminder

**Geopolitical Shock Days demand patience, not activity.**

The professionals trading on Hormuz news today have real-time intelligence, institutional hedging books, and orders of magnitude more capital than you. Your edge is NOT in calling macro direction faster than them. Your edge is in waiting for the chart to show you a clean setup at a defined level, then executing with discipline.

The first moves on a shock day are violent, often fake-out, and then reverse. The second or third setup — after structure forms — is where the real opportunity lies for a disciplined day trader.

**Reminder**: You need one good trade to have a winning day. You don't need to "participate" in every candle. If it doesn't match your playbook, your hands stay off the mouse.

If VIX is spiking and the candles are wild: **it is completely valid to sit out entirely and watch.** Observation is free. Forced trades are expensive.
