# Risk Management Rules

## Account: $1,100 | Broker: AMP Futures | Instrument: MES

These rules are NON-NEGOTIABLE. Breaking them is the fastest way to blow a small account.

---

## Position Sizing

| Rule | Value | Reasoning |
|------|-------|-----------|
| **Max contracts** | 1 MES | Small account = 1 contract only |
| **Risk per trade** | 2% = $22 | Conservative for small account |
| **Max stop distance** | 5 points ($25) | Hard ceiling |
| **Default stop** | 4 points ($20) | Standard setup |
| **Tight stop** | 2-3 points ($10-15) | Scalp setups |
| **Min reward:risk** | 1.5:1 | No trade below this ratio |

### Position Size Calculator
With $1,100 and 2% risk ($22):
- 2-point stop: 1 contract, risk = $10 ✅
- 3-point stop: 1 contract, risk = $15 ✅
- 4-point stop: 1 contract, risk = $20 ✅
- 5-point stop: 1 contract, risk = $25 ⚠️ (slightly over 2%, acceptable)
- 6+ point stop: **DO NOT TAKE THE TRADE** ❌

## Daily Limits

| Limit | Value | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| **Max daily loss** | $55 (5%) | STOP trading for the day. No exceptions. |
| **Max daily trades** | 4 | Quality over quantity |
| **Consecutive losses** | 2 in a row | Take 30-min break minimum |
| **Near daily limit** | Within $15 of max loss | Take only A+ setups |

### Daily Loss Circuit Breakers
1. **-$22 (1 loss)**: Normal. Continue if setup quality is high.
2. **-$40 (2 losses)**: Take a 30-minute break. Walk away from screen. Come back only if you feel calm and focused.
3. **-$55 (daily limit)**: DONE. Close platform. No more trades today. Review in the evening.

## Overnight Rules

- **NO OVERNIGHT POSITIONS.** Period.
- Overnight margin ($2,455) exceeds account ($1,100)
- All positions must be flat before 22:30 CET (30 min buffer before halt)
- If you forget and the daily halt approaches, close immediately regardless of P&L

## Commission Awareness

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Round trip cost | $0.84 |
| As % of $20 profit (4pt target) | 4.2% |
| As % of $10 profit (2pt target) | 8.4% |
| Break-even after commission | 0.67 ticks (0.17 points) |

Commission is small but compounds. Track it. On a $1,100 account, 4 trades/day = $3.36/day in commissions = ~$67/month.

## Pre-Trade Risk Checklist

Before EVERY trade, answer these:

1. ☐ Is my stop defined? (NEVER enter without a stop)
2. ☐ Is the stop ≤ 5 points? ($25 max risk)
3. ☐ Is the reward:risk ≥ 1.5:1?
4. ☐ Am I within my daily trade count? (≤ 4)
5. ☐ Am I within my daily loss limit? (< $55 lost)
6. ☐ Is this a high-probability setup from my playbook?
7. ☐ Am I trading during a valid session? (London AM or NY PM)
8. ☐ Are there any news events in the next 15 minutes?

If ANY answer is NO → **DO NOT TRADE.**

## The #1 Rule

**Protect your capital.** With $1,100 you cannot afford to be careless. A 50% drawdown ($550 loss) requires a 100% return to recover. Trade small, trade smart, survive long enough to improve.
